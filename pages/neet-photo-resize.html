NEET UG 2026 Photo Size Requirements

Official NEET UG 2026 Photo & Signature Requirements

Photo Requirements:

  • Dimensions: 354 × 472 pixels (3.5 cm × 4.5 cm)
  • File Size: 10 KB to 200 KB
  • Format: JPEG
  • Background: White or light colored
  • Face should be clearly visible

Signature Requirements:

  • Dimensions: 236 × 118 pixels
  • File Size: 4 KB to 30 KB
  • Format: JPEG
  • Black or blue ink on white paper
