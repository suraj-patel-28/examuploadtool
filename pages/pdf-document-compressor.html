Common Issues & Solutions

Q: My PDF is too large (over 300 KB)?
A: Rescan the document at lower DPI (150 instead of 300) or use black & white mode.

Q: Can I compress multiple pages?
A: Yes, but ensure all pages are in a single PDF file before uploading.

Q: Will compression reduce quality?
A: Text documents remain readable. Avoid over-compression for photos.

Q: Which exams require PDF documents?
A: Almost all - GATE, JEE, NEET, UPSC, SSC, Banking exams, etc.
