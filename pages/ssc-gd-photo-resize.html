SSC GD Photo Size Requirements

Official SSC GD Photo & Signature Requirements

Photo Requirements:

  • Dimensions: 100 × 120 pixels
  • File Size: 20 KB to 50 KB
  • Format: JPEG
  • Recent passport size photo
  • Clear face visibility required

Signature Requirements:

  • Dimensions: 140 × 60 pixels
  • File Size: 10 KB to 20 KB
  • Format: JPEG
  • Black or blue ink on white paper
